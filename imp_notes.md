# FinSights - Key Insights

## 1. Behavioral Fingerprints
- **Active Everyday Spenders (51.5%)** - 91 txns, 42 merchants, $4,249 spend. BSB's core.
- **Lending-Engaged Loyal (17%)** - similar spend pattern, deepest tenure (17.5 yrs), 99% have loans.
- **Low-Frequency Big Spenders (17%)** - 12 txns, 7 merchants, $161 avg transaction. Few but large.
- **Low-Activity Digital Users (14.4%)** - 6 txns, 4 merchants, $126 total. Barely engaged.

## 2. Product Engagement
- Lending-Engaged: 99.2% have loans, avg 1.28 loans - this defines them.
- Active Everyday: **0% have loans** - 30,244 customers with high engagement, zero cross-sell. Biggest growth opportunity.
- Credit card penetration ~2-3% across all segments

## 3. Where the Money Goes
- Active Everyday & Lending-Engaged spend identically: grocery 22%, dining 10%, retail 13%.
- Low-Activity Digital: 11.7% digital, 11.3% telecom - subscriptions dominate.
- Low-Frequency Big: 20.2% other, 15.3% financial, 9.3% telecom - **bill payers, not shoppers.**

## 4. Geographic Story
- "Out-of-state" isn't travel - it's bills routed through corporate HQs (Walmart AR, Apple CA, State Farm IL).
- Bangor dominates all segments, Portland is #2.
- Low-Activity Digital: more out-of-state txns than in-state (56% vs 44%).

## 5. Local Merchant Opportunity
- Active Everyday shops at 1,130+ Maine-only merchants ($53.7M) - already the local economy engine.
- Key local names: Hannaford, Central Maine Power, Dead River, Maritime Farms, Hammond Lumber.
- Low-Activity Digital: only 334 local merchants. 

## 6. Recurring vs One-Time
- Low-Activity Digital: 22% recurring - card relationship is basically autopay (Apple, Netflix, PlayStation).
- Low-Frequency Big: 17% recurring, $104 avg - large autopay bills (Spectrum, insurance, QuickBooks).
- Active Everyday: 11% recurring - mostly intentional one-time swipes.

## 7. Demographics Don't Drive Segments
- Ages 46-57, tenure 13-17 yrs - similar across 3 of 4 segments.
- Lending-Engaged stands alone: oldest (57.5), longest tenure, highest primary rate.
